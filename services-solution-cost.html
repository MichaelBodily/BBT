Better Lobby Solution Cost

Better Lobby Solution is an affordable multiple-module application for branch visitor queuing, appointment handling and workforce management.

Cost varies based on the size of your branch network and the modules purchased. Licensing is available as either a local installation in your data center or as an ASP-Hosted solution in the Better Branches' data center.
